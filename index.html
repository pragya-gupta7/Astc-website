About us

We at ARKA are driven by a desire to explore and understand the working and the mysteries of our universe. ARKA, or the Association for Research and Knowledge in Astronomy is the official Astronomy and Physics club of IIT(ISM) Dhanbad. The club was established on the 5th of December 2017 under the able leadership of six esteemed seniors of the batch of 2017.

ARKA was founded to provide a platform for students, amateurs, and hobbyists alike to expand upon their knowledge of our universe. To help its members gain technical expertise in astronomy, ARKA frequently organizes competitions, seminars, space talks, and events with prominent scientists and researchers. The club also has its very own state-of-the-art Dobsonian telescope to explore the marvels of the night sky. We are a team of die-hard space enthusiasts who admire the excellence of astronomy and space science and seek to spread its magnificence around us.
